KalKulVest is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

3. Children’s Privacy

  • Our App does not target children under the age of 13.

Since the App does not collect any information, it complies with COPPA and Google Play’s Families Policy requirements.

No user data is shared with anyone.

5. Data Security

  • All data remains local on your device.
